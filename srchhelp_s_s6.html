Journal, Magazine & Newspaper Title Search

Type the Title, then press ENTER:

This search will only find journals, magazines and newspapers which the library owns. It will not find journal articles. To find individual articles, select the appropriate database from On-Line Indexes & Databases.

Type as much or as little of the EXACT TITLE as you want.

for example

  • journal of counseling psychology
  • journal of counseling psy
  • journal of the philosphy of sport

If your search is unsuccessful, try a TITLE or KEYWORD search.
